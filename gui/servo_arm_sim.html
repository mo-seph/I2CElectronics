Geometry notes 
a1 = MIN_BASE + p2 · (MAX_BASE − MIN_BASE)
α  = −a1   (world math angle)
U  = (d2·cos(a1), −d2·sin(a1))
φ  = (p1 − 0.5) · 2 · MAX_ANGLE
T  = (d1·tan(φ), d1)
βreq = atan2(T.y − U.y, T.x − U.x)
a2 = βreq + a1 − 90°   (clamped to [0°, 180°])

At a1=90° the arm points south (away from wall). At a2=90° the laser points back along the arm (opposite to arm direction). Wall is horizontal at y = d1. Target φ measured from straight-up.
